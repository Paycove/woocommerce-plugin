Paycove is a powerful WooCommerce plugin that streamlines checkout, payment processing, and invoice generation.

== Description ==

**Paycove for WooCommerce** is an all-in-one payment automation tool designed to enhance your checkout process with flexible payment options. Whether you're selling products or services, Paycove makes it easy to offer one-time payments, installment plans, and dynamic checkout flows.

### Features:
- **Customizable Checkout** – Embed a seamless checkout flow within WooCommerce.
- **Flexible Payment Plans** – Let customers choose from one-time payments, deposits, or installment plans.
- **Invoice Generation** – Automatically create detailed invoices with tax and payment breakdowns.
- **Global Payment Methods** – Accept ACH, credit cards, and other payment options.
- **WooCommerce Sync** – Keep orders and payments in sync with WooCommerce's order management system.
- **Simple Pricing** – Transparent transaction fees with an optional yearly subscription.

### Why Choose Paycove?
Paycove simplifies the payment process for businesses by automating checkout, reducing friction for customers, and ensuring seamless transaction handling. Whether you're running a small shop or a large-scale store, Paycove helps you manage payments efficiently.

== Installation ==

1. Download and install the plugin through the WordPress Plugin Directory or upload the ZIP file manually.
2. Activate the plugin from the WordPress admin dashboard.
3. Go to **WooCommerce > Settings > Paycove** and enter your Paycove API credentials.
4. Customize your payment options and checkout settings to fit your business needs.
5. Save changes and start accepting payments through Paycove!

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= What payment methods are supported? =
Paycove supports multiple payment methods, including credit cards, ACH transfers, and payment plans.

= How do I configure Paycove in WooCommerce? =
After installing the plugin, navigate to **WooCommerce > Settings > Paycove** and follow the setup instructions to connect your account.

= Can I offer payment plans to customers? =
Yes! Paycove allows merchants to set up flexible installment plans, enabling customers to pay over time.

= Is Paycove secure? =
Absolutely. Paycove follows industry-standard security protocols, including encrypted payment processing and PCI compliance.

= Where can I get support? =
For any issues or questions, visit [https://paycove.io](https://paycove.io) and reach out to our support team.
